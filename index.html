Privacy Policy for Window Resizer Extension

Effective Date: November 19, 2024
Last Updated: November 19, 2024

Overview

Window Resizer is a browser extension that allows users to resize their browser window to predefined or custom dimensions. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

Information We Collect

We do NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

The only data stored locally on your device is:

  • Dark Mode Preference: Your choice of light or dark theme for the extension interface

This preference is stored locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API and never leaves your device.

What We Don't Collect

We explicitly DO NOT collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, address, etc.)
  • Browsing history or activity
  • Website URLs you visit
  • Window sizes you use
  • Location data
  • Cookies or tracking data
  • Any analytics or usage statistics
  • IP addresses
  • Device information beyond what the browser provides

How We Use Information

The dark mode preference stored locally is used solely to:

  • Remember your theme choice (light or dark mode)
  • Provide a consistent user interface when you reopen the extension

This data is stored only on your device and is never transmitted to us or any third party.

Data Storage

  • All data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's chrome.storage.sync API
  • No data is sent to external servers
  • No remote databases are used
  • Data remains private to you and your browser

Third-Party Access

We do not:

  • Share any data with third parties
  • Use third-party analytics services
  • Include third-party tracking code
  • Integrate with external services or APIs

Permissions Used

Our extension requests the following permission:

Storage Permission

  • Purpose: To save your dark mode theme preference locally
  • Data Stored: Only your light/dark mode choice
  • Data Sharing: None - stored locally only

Data Security

Since we don't collect or transmit any data, your information remains secure on your device. The extension operates entirely within your browser without any external connections.

Children's Privacy

Our extension does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The extension is safe for all ages.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • You can clear the dark mode preference by uninstalling the extension
  • You can reset preferences by toggling the dark mode switch
  • No data exists outside your local browser storage

Contact Information

If you have questions or concerns about this privacy policy, you can:

  • Leave a review on the Chrome Web Store
  • Contact us through the Chrome Web Store support section

Compliance

This extension complies with:

  • Chrome Web Store Developer Program Policies
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
  • Chrome Extension Privacy Requirements

Summary

In simple terms:

  • We don't collect your data
  • We don't track you
  • We don't share anything
  • Only your theme preference is saved locally on your device
  • Your privacy is fully protected
